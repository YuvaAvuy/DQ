Dulquer Salman

Dulquer Salmaan is an Indian actor, playback singer and film producer who predominantly works in Malayalam and Tamil films in addition to Telugu and Hindi films. Dulquer is a recipient of several awards including, four Filmfare Awards South and a Kerala State Film Award.
